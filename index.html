Baja California

Baja California is a Mexican state on the Baja California Peninsula that borders the U.S. state of California. The landscape spans mountains and beaches along the Pacific Ocean and the Gulf of California. Cities near the U.S. border include Tijuana, known as a commercial and bohemian destination, as well as the city of Rosarito, with its wide beaches along the Pacific.
